Vote ? Look up a real-world currency exchanger (e.g., Uniswap) and choose the JLC <=> USD exchange rate at the point when the current phase started. If no currency exchanger supports the JLC <=> USD conversion yet, choose the oracle level 5.

As a result of the voting at phase N, 10% of your coin balance is deposited to the ACB. The deposited coins will be returned to your wallet with some reward at phase N+2 as long as you vote for a correct exchange rate at phase N+1 and phase N+2. See help to learn the mechanism more.
