Vote ? You can get a reward by voting for the ``truth'' exchange rate every phase. See help to learn more.
Current oracle level: The oracle level you vote for in the current phase. Choose the oracle level that corresponds to the exchange rate as of when the current phase started. If you intend to not vote in the current phase, leave it empty.
Salt: The salt number (a 256 bit unsigned integer) to protect your vote in the current phase. Use different salt numbers every vote and keep it confidential until you reveal the vote in the next phase. If you intend to not vote in the current phase, leave it empty.
Reveal the oracle level in your previous vote: The oracle level you voted for in the previous phase. If you did not vote in the previous phase, leave it empty.
Reveal the salt in your previous vote: The salt number you used in the previous phase. If you did not vote in the previous phase, leave it empty.
As a result of voting, 10% of your coin balance is deposited to the ACB. The deposited coins will be returned to your wallet later only if 1) you voted for an oracle level that is within one level from the ``truth'' oracle level and 2) you revealed the vote correctly. Otherwise, you will lose the deposited coins. Therefore, it is important to vote and reveal every phase.
